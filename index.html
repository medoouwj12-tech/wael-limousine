About Us

A Decade of Refined Service

Wael Limousine began over a decade ago with a single Mercedes and a simple promise: deliver a chauffeur experience worthy of the name. Today, our fleet has grown to nine premium vehicles — but the promise hasn't changed.

From the gleaming towers of the New Administrative Capital to the timeless stones of the Pyramids of Giza, we've had the privilege of moving thousands of clients — diplomats, executives, honeymooners, and pilgrims — across every corner of Egypt.

Every chauffeur on our team is handpicked, English-speaking, and trained in the art of discreet VIP service. Every vehicle is detailed to showroom condition before each ride. Every booking is handled with a single WhatsApp message — no apps, no waiting, no hassle.
